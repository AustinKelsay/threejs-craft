Movement & Camera

  • Click to capture mouse and look around
  • WASD or ‚Üë‚Üì‚Üê‚Üí to move
  • SHIFT to sprint

Building

  • 0-7 to select items (changes with location)
  • Q/E to rotate object preview
  • B to place selected object
  • SPACE to remove object you're looking at

Resources & Inventory

  • Click trees/rocks with fists to gather wood/stone
  • I to open/close inventory
  • In inventory: click a resource, then G to drop 1
  • Click dropped items to pick them up

Combat & Creatures

  • Fists (slot 0) punch creatures and break objects
  • Punch spiders to defend yourself
  • Hearts at top center show your health
  • Click dragons to mount them
  • Backspace to dismount

Save & Load

  • F5 to save your game
  • F9 to load your game
